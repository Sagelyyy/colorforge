This is a web app to colorize text in the MUD Legends of The Jedi.

# 🌈 Legends of The Jedi Text Colorizer 🌈

Welcome, future Jedi (or Sith, no judgment here) to the _ultimate_ color previewer for your Legends of The Jedi text adventures! 🚀👾

## What is this magic? 🎩🔮

This is a Svelte application designed to help you visualize and customize the color output of your in-game text for Legends of The Jedi. Easily adjust the color of any part of your text, apply palettes, and even import and export your color groups as a `.json` file!

## 🌟 Features

- 💅 **Beautiful, Intuitive UI**: Because we know you have a galaxy to save (or conquer), we've made the process as smooth as lightsaber's blade.
- 🎨 **Custom Color Selection**: Mix and match to find the perfect shade for your character's dialogues, narratives, or battle cries.
- 👥 **Swatch Group Creation**: Want a color theme for your Sith Lord? Or a palette for that peaceful planet you're visiting? We got you covered.
- 💾 **Import/Export**: Traveling through space and time (or just different machines)? No worries! Carry your colors with you.
- 🔎 **Live Preview**: What you see is what you get. Your colors, as they'll appear in the game, live and in technicolor.

## 🤖 May The Colors Be With You

Whether you're exploring new star systems or mastering the ways of the force, we hope this text colorizer adds a splash of color to your journey.
